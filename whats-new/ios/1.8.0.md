# New Features
- **Intro And Outro Skip Lengths** — Choose how much to skip at the start and end of each episode per podcast; Pro and Premium unlock the feature, and Mimir Plus now explains skip lengths alongside other subscription benefits.

# Improvements
- **Now Playing Scrubber** — Seeks apply when you lift your finger from the scrubber, the time readouts stay lined up with playback, and the bar feels steadier while dragging.
- **Sharing Podcasts** — The share sheet is smoother and behaves better when a show has no website link in its feed.
- **Downloads Sorting** — Your preferred sort order in the downloads list is remembered.

# Bug Fixes
- Fixed incorrect interface scaling on iOS 26.
